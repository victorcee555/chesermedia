About CHESER MEDIA

Discover our mission to celebrate the Cherubim and Seraphim Church.

Welcome to CHESER MEDIA

We are a dedicated Public Relations (PR) outfit focused on enhancing the image and telling the untold stories of the Cherubim and Seraphim Church (C & S Church). Our mission is to provide a fresh perspective, correct misconceptions, and showcase the rich history, vibrant culture, and untapped talents of the C & S Church.

Our Mission

At CHESER MEDIA, we believe that every story deserves to be told. Many are unfamiliar with the true history and deep spiritual significance of the C & S Church. Our primary mission is to bridge the gap between the church's rich heritage and the broader public through innovative media and communication strategies. By doing so, we aim to provide the C & S Church with the recognition it deserves and foster a deeper understanding of its values, beliefs, and impact.

Why We Exist

The C & S Church has always been a community deeply rooted in faith, love, and unity. However, many are unaware of its significance, history, and contributions to both spirituality and society. CHESER MEDIA exists to fill that gap. Our goal is to promote accurate portrayals of the church, dispelling myths and providing a platform for its story to be told authentically.
